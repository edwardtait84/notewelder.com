Our Mission

NoteWelder is an independent online music directory and publisher! Our focus is on producing arrangements of the greatest orchestral works, for the accessibility and convenience of the solo piano. Our music is available for purchase in both online and printed format.

We are very proud to hold exclusive selling rights to Edward Tait's solo piano arrangement of Holst's The Planets suite, the first of its kind! We support composers and arrangers studying at prestigious conservatoires such as the Royal Academy of Music and the Royal Northern College of Music.

Our catalogue of music is growing all the time...
